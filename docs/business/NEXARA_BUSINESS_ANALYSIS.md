## 1. Executive Summary

Bengal Bound Ltd is launching **Nexara**, the world's first compliance-certified AI-as-Employee marketplace from Bangladesh. Nexara enables SMEs globally to hire autonomous AI agents at 10–50x lower cost than human equivalents, with every agent supervised by an always-on compliance engine.

**Funding Ask (Seed):** $150,000 USD
**Use of Funds:** 12-month runway (team + infrastructure) + marketing + legal
**Target Break-Even:** Month 14 at 200 clients

## 3. Competitive Analysis

| Competitor | What They Do | Our Advantage |
|---|---|---|
| **ChatGPT / Claude** | General AI chatbot | We're a workforce, not a chatbot. Agents do jobs autonomously. |
| **Zapier / Make** | Workflow automation | We reason and decide, not just trigger-action. Inspector = compliance they don't have. |
| **Copy.ai / Jasper** | AI content tools | Single-purpose vs our full Content Architect + 29 other agents. |
| **Fiverr / Upwork** | Human freelancers | 10–50x cheaper, 24/7, no sick days, fully auditable. |
| **Salesforce Einstein** | Enterprise CRM AI | Enterprise-only, $500+/mo. We serve SMEs at $18/mo. |
| **BelugaAI / AgentGPT** | AI agent platforms | No compliance engine. No KYB. No jurisdiction-aware enforcement. |
| **Local BD IT firms** | Custom software | One-time build vs SaaS recurring revenue. |

### Key Differentiators (Unfair Advantages)
1. **Inspector** — Only AI platform with always-on built-in global compliance gate
2. **Bangladesh-first** — First-mover in BD's underserved SME AI market
3. **30+ agents** — Widest roster of specialized AI employees
4. **Cost** — BD-based team = 5–10x lower burn than US/EU competitors
5. **KYB gate** — Only AI marketplace that verifies clients before activation

## 4. SWOT Analysis

### Strengths
- Strong technical team with AI + Django + Flutter expertise
- First-mover in Bangladesh AI-as-Employee market
- Modular architecture allows rapid agent addition
- Inspector = built-in trust and compliance moat
- Low operational cost (BD-based team)

### Weaknesses
- Limited brand recognition outside Bangladesh initially
- App store submissions take time (Apple review 1–3 weeks)
- Dependency on Google Gemini API (mitigated by abstraction layer)
- Need Wyoming LLC for Stripe (short-term fix, manageable)

### Opportunities
- Bangladesh IT sector growing 20%+ annually
- Global demand for affordable AI workforce tools
- AI Act compliance gap — competitors not ready
- White-label programme → agencies as resellers
- Own GPU server → zero marginal AI cost at scale

### Threats
- OpenAI / Google launching similar products
- Bangladesh regulatory changes (Digital Security Act evolution)
- Exchange rate risk (BDT/USD)
- Client data breach (mitigated by Inspector + encryption)
