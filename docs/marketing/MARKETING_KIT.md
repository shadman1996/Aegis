## Positioning Statement

**For:** SMEs, digital agencies, clinics, real estate firms, and growing businesses

**Who need:** Skilled professionals across multiple departments but can't afford full teams

**Nexara is:** An AI-as-Employee marketplace

**That:** Provides 30+ autonomous AI agents who do real jobs, work 24/7, and cost 10–50x less than human equivalents

**Unlike:** ChatGPT, Zapier, or generic AI tools that require heavy setup and human operation

**Because:** Every agent is supervised by Inspector — the only platform with built-in global compliance enforcement
